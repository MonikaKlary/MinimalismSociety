The Exciting New Minimalism Society

Our society aims to create a space for like-minded people to express and share ideas around minimalism, as well as introduce individuals to this amazing lifestyle. We believe that minimalism comes in different shapes and forms, and we as a society encourage people to join us and share what minimalism means to them. We also organise a bunch of activities, talks and events that promote this lifestyle we are so passionate about, and opportunities to volunteer within our society, and outside of university. Join us today, and start your own journey!
